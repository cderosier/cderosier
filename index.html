About Me

Grounded in aviation.
Driven by understanding people.

I'm Charles DeRosier IV — a ramp agent at Los Angeles International Airport working for JetBlue Airlines. My work demands precision, physical stamina, and seamless teamwork to keep aircraft moving safely and on schedule in one of the country's busiest airports.

Before aviation, I competed as a Division I football player — first at Arkansas State University and then at the University of Northern Colorado. That experience instilled discipline, mental toughness, and a team-first mindset that shapes how I approach every challenge.

Academically, I've pursued sociology, political science, and psychology across three institutions. I'm genuinely fascinated by human behavior — how people communicate, make decisions, and adapt under pressure. Being bilingual in English and Spanish only deepens my ability to connect with people from all walks of life.
